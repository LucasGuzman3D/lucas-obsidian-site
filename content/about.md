Creative director with 15 years building at the intersection of motion, real-time 3D, and product systems. One person delivering what previously required a full creative team. AI does the volume and iteration. I do the judgment, the quality gate, and the creative direction.

## Background

Started as a self-taught 3D artist in Argentina at 12. Hired remotely by an Amsterdam fashion company at 17. Presented my first major production during a fashion show in Amsterdam at 18. All before any formal training existed for what I was doing.

That path went through fashion visualization, indie games, and eventually Capitola Digital, where I directed VR experiences, AR marketing, motion capture, and medical simulations. In the blockchain era, I designed for crypto companies and built my own NFT art practice. At Sophon, a DeFi startup, I directed the full creative output: product psychology, gamification mechanics, AI content pipelines, and brand systems. Designing how users understand and interact with complex financial products.
