In october of 2023 I joined a team of game  and blockchain devs for the EBC Hackaton in Barcelona. After 48hs the result was GuildHUB. The Guild Management Tool.

THE GUILD MANAGEMENT TOOL

GuildHUB is a guild management tool operating on the Coreum Blockchain. It features a backend that allows users to log in using their Coreum wallets. In this space, the Guild leader can manage guild members, vote on which games and raids to tackle next, give away NFTs earned during raids, and much more.

In addition to the backend UI, GuildHUB members can access our exclusive virtual space. This multiplayer interactive experience lets members customize their avatars and enter their own 3D space. Here, they can utilize the same features as the backend UI, including an NFT trophy room that directly gathers data from the blockchain.
