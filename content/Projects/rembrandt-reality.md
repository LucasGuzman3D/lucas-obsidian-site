**Role:** Senior 3D Artist at Capitola Digital. Actor direction and 3D production for an award-winning AR experience.

![[rembrandt-Capitola_RembrandtReality_Image3.jpg]]

Rembrandt Reality lets you step inside Rembrandt's "The Anatomical Lesson of Dr. Nicolaes Tulp." The 17th-century scene comes to life around you. Figures, instruments, the anatomy theater. All reconstructed in 3D and placed in your physical space through AR.

![[rembrandt-ali_in_rembrandt.jpg]]
## What I did

![[rembrandt-jeffrey_rembrand.jpg]]

Directed the 3D scanning sessions with real actors. Oversaw the optimization of those scans into production-ready 3D models. Delivered everything packaged for AR devices. The tricky part: keeping historical accuracy and visual fidelity while making it all run in real-time on mobile hardware. That's two very different skill sets in one problem.

## Impact

Won the **ADCN Dutch Creativity Award 2020**. One of the Netherlands' most respected creative industry recognitions. Proved that real-time 3D and AR could serve cultural storytelling, not just entertainment or marketing.
