## The Problem

Sophon ran a weekly livestream called Aurastreams, featuring rotating guests discussing fintech, marketing, and emerging technology. Each episode needed a unique announcement visual for X, but guest confirmations often came just 24 to 48 hours before airtime. The brand was known for never repeating itself visually. No templates. No recycled layouts. The question wasn't "how do we make a nice graphic." It was: **how do we build a creative system that produces unique, on-brand visuals at speed, without a design template?**

## Alternatives I Evaluated

**Option A. The Branded template:** Sophon's blue sky, the guest and the host... Meh.
![[old-livestream.webp]]

**Option B. Custom illustration per episode.** Beautiful in theory. With a 48-hour turnaround and no illustrator? Good luck.

**Option C. AI image generation as a creative pipeline.** New models had just learned to take real faces and drop them into fully prompted scenes. So instead of templating the design, I templated the process. Same pipeline every time. Completely different output every time.

## The Decision

I chose Option C and designed the creative direction around a single concept: **show the hosts together in the same scene, even though they're on opposite sides of the world.** Every episode became a fictional hangout. Rooftops. Retro arcade basements. Busy downtown cafés. The AI generation handled the heavy lifting of scene creation. 
![[ai-aura-tests.webp]]

This gave us three things at once: **Speed.** **Uniqueness.** **Brand alignment.**

![[ai-aura-progress.webp]]
![[ai-aura-style.gif]]
## The Result

Announcement posts consistently outperformed Sophon's standard branded content on X. Audience engagement spiked. Comments regularly referenced the visuals themselves ("this post has all the Aura"), which almost never happens with promotional content during a bear market. The format became a signature of the show and was sustained across the full run of episodes.

![[ai-aura-result.webp]]

More importantly: I built a **repeatable creative pipeline** that anyone on the team could brief into. The system outlasted any single episode. That's the difference between making a good image and building a creative operation.
