**Role:** 3D production, character design, and multiplayer experience. Concept to working prototype in 48 hours.

October 2023. A team of 5: game devs, blockchain devs, and me. European Blockchain Convention Hackathon in Barcelona. Open brief: build something useful on the Coreum blockchain. **We built GuildHUB. Won first place.**

## What we built

![[landing_layers_infographic.png]]

GuildHUB is a guild management platform on Coreum. Wallet login, member management, voting, NFT distribution. All the gaming guild management features you could possibly need. 

The part I owned was the fun part: a 3D multiplayer space where guild members could actually hang out, interact, and do all the same management stuff but inside an immersive environment.

![[bts2.gif]]

Character customization system. Multiplayer hub. An NFT trophy room pulling data straight from the blockchain. All modeled, animated, networked, and integrated in 48 hours. Placeholder character parts because, well, 48 hours.

![[vault.gif]]
## The result

Hackathons strip everything down to one question: **can you ship?** No time to overthink. No room for scope creep. This one proved I could direct the creative and technical side of a multiplayer 3D experience from zero, coordinate across disciplines, and deliver something polished enough to win. In two days.
